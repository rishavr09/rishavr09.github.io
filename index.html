Privacy-Preserving Medical Data Analysis using Homomorphic Encryption (Ongoing)

This research focuses on applying homomorphic encryption to enable secure analysis of sensitive medical data without compromising patient privacy. By utilizing Microsoft SEAL, encrypted datasets are processed directly, allowing computations such as statistical analysis (e.g., average age, disease distribution) to be performed on ciphertexts—eliminating the need for decryption.
